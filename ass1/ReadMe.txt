代码执行方法：
（1）安装python3和jieba
（2）将code.py、Chinese-stop-words.txt和lily放置于同一目录下，目录树如下所示：
├── Chinese-stop-words.txt
├── code.py
├── lily
│   ├── Basketball.txt
│   ├── D_Computer.txt
│   ├── FleaMarket.txt
│   ├── Girls.txt
│   ├── JobExpress.txt
│   ├── Mobile.txt
│   ├── Stock.txt
│   ├── V_Suggestions.txt
│   ├── WarAndPeace.txt
│   └── WorldFootball.txt
（3）执行python3 code.py，将在result目录下看到输出
